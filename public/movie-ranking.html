Best Movies According to Urooj

My top 3 Movies of all the times

3 idiots

3 Idiots is a movie directed by Rajkumar Hirani that explores themes of passion, purpose, and integrity. The movie explores these themes by questioning conventional norms and inspiring people to follow their true calling.

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

Arjun's fear of water serves as a metaphor for the fears that hold us back in life. Despite his initial reluctance, he pushes himself to face his fear head-on by engaging in scuba diving. This teaches us the importance of stepping out of our comfort zones and conquering our fears to experience personal growth.

Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani

The film Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani was released in 2014. It has become a cult classic among the Indian youth. Deepika Padukone and Ranbir Kapoor played the roles of Naina and Bunny in this film. In May 2012, the cast filmed in Udaipur. Depicting the royal city of Udaipur in its full glory, Oberoi Udaivilas played the perfect host to the team of Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani.
